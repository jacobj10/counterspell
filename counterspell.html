What is this farce you may ask?

There is a room full of (Dungeons and Dragons 5th edition) casters, who conviniently live in a morally decisive world which places them into two groups, dare I say "teams". Ever wondered what would happen if a spell was cast and the whole (re)action economy was used to counterspell? Well look no further than the Counterspell! simulator. Simply fill out a the info for each team, and hit enter to add more players. Happy reacts!
